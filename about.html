About Us

One institution for every learning stage.

JGS Group of Institutes in Andheri East brings school, junior college, B.Tech, postgraduate learning, portals and placement preparation into one student-centered ecosystem.

Talk to Admissions▶ Explore campus
What is JGS?

A KG to PG institution offering school education, junior college, engineering branches, postgraduate programs and student services.

Where is it located?

JGS is located in Andheri East, Mumbai, Maharashtra, with academic blocks, labs, library spaces and student zones.

What makes it different?

The institute connects learning, mentoring, placements and digital portals so students and parents get clear visibility.

Which engineering branches are offered?

AI & ML, Computer Science, ECE, EEE, Mechanical and Civil, with 50 seats per branch.

Our Mission

Guided learning with real outcomes.

We focus on confidence, discipline, project-based learning, communication and career readiness. From early classrooms to graduation day, JGS gives students structured support and modern tools.

What JGS provides

Academic Continuity

Students can begin in school and continue through junior college, undergraduate engineering and postgraduate pathways without losing institutional support.

Mentorship System

Faculty mentors track attendance, internal marks, assignments, projects, internships and student concerns through structured review points.

Parent Visibility

Parents can monitor attendance, marks, fee status, meeting bookings, teacher messages, emergency contacts and academic calendar updates.

Faculty Operations

Faculty use a separate portal to mark attendance, upload marks, post notices, message parents, track syllabus and handle HR tasks.
