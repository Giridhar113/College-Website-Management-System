School Curriculum

KG to Class 10 learning plan

Pre-Primary: Nursery to KG

Focuses on language readiness, social confidence, basic numeracy, motor skills and creative expression.

  • Phonics, storytelling and picture reading
  • Numbers, shapes, colors and patterns
  • Music, movement, art and play-based activities
Activity BasedFoundations

Primary: Classes 1 to 5

Builds reading, writing, arithmetic, environmental awareness and classroom discipline.

  • English, Hindi/Marathi, Mathematics and EVS
  • Computer basics, art, sports and general knowledge
  • Weekly homework, reading practice and small projects
Core SkillsProjects

Middle School: Classes 6 to 8

Introduces deeper subject learning, science thinking, social awareness and independent study habits.

  • Science, mathematics, languages and social studies
  • Computer applications, lab demonstrations and quizzes
  • Clubs, presentations and practical assignments
Concept ClarityClubs

Secondary: Classes 9 and 10

Prepares students for board-level performance with structured revision and regular assessment.

  • Mathematics, Science, Social Science and languages
  • Chapter tests, pre-board exams and doubt-solving sessions
  • Career orientation for Intermediate and degree pathways
Board ReadyMentoring

Assessment Pattern

Progress is tracked through unit tests, projects, notebooks, oral work, activities and term exams.

  • Monthly class tests and homework review
  • Parent updates through notices and meetings
  • Remedial support for students needing extra help
ReportsRemedial

Life Skills

  • Morning assembly, events and cultural activities
  • Sports, health awareness and leadership tasks
  • Library reading and responsible technology use
