Full Curriculum

School, Intermediate and College tracks

School Curriculum

KG to Class 10 focuses on language, mathematics, science, social studies, computer basics, art, sports and values.

  • Pre-primary activity learning
  • Primary literacy and numeracy
  • Class 9 and 10 board preparation
KG-10Board Ready

Intermediate Curriculum

Class 11 and 12 include Science and Commerce pathways with practicals, projects, revision tests and admission counseling.

  • PCM / PCB / Commerce options
  • Practical records and pre-board exams
  • Career counseling for degree choices
XI-XIICareer

B.Tech Common First Year

All engineering students build foundations in mathematics, physics, chemistry, programming, engineering graphics and workshop practice.

  • Coding and problem solving
  • Basic electrical and electronics exposure
  • Communication and professional skills
FoundationLabs

Branch Core

From second year onward, students study branch-specific subjects for AI & ML, CSE, ECE, EEE, Mechanical or Civil.

  • Theory papers and tutorials
  • Laboratory work and records
  • Mini projects and seminars
CoreProjects

Electives and Certifications

Advanced electives and skill tracks help students prepare for internships, research, startups and placements.

  • AI, cloud, IoT, robotics and infrastructure topics
  • Aptitude, coding and communication workshops
  • Industry certification guidance
ElectivesPlacement

Final Year Outcome

Students complete a major project, placement training, interview preparation and career documentation.

  • Capstone project and review panels
  • Resume, mock interview and drive registration
  • Hall ticket, ID card and dashboard support
