Common admission questions

What courses are available at JGS?

JGS supports school education, intermediate streams, and B.Tech branches including AI&ML, CS, Civil, ECE, Mechanical, and EEE.

How do I apply for admission?

Use the Apply page, submit student details, then complete counseling, document verification, and fee confirmation.

Is transport or hostel support available?

The admissions team can guide families about transport, day-scholar support, and available student facilities.

How do students access the portal?

Students use the Student ID and password issued by the institution. For login help, contact the office.

How do staff access the portal?

Authorized staff should use official staff credentials issued by JGS. Never share your password or OTP with anyone.
