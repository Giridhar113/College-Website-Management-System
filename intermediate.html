Science Curriculum

Science stream groups

MPC - Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry

Best for engineering, B.Tech, architecture, statistics, computer science, defence technical entries, and pure science pathways.

First Year Subjects
  • English and second language
  • Mathematics IA: algebra, functions, trigonometry, vectors, and coordinate basics
  • Mathematics IB: calculus foundation, geometry, probability basics, and analytical practice
  • Physics: units, motion, laws of motion, work-energy, heat, waves, and optics basics
  • Chemistry: atomic structure, bonding, states of matter, solutions, and organic basics
  • Physics and Chemistry practical records, lab observations, and weekly problem practice
Second Year Subjects
  • Mathematics IIA: complex numbers, quadratic equations, permutations, probability, and calculus
  • Mathematics IIB: coordinate geometry, circles, parabola, ellipse, hyperbola, and vector algebra
  • Physics: electricity, magnetism, modern physics, semiconductors, and communication systems
  • Chemistry: thermodynamics, electrochemistry, chemical kinetics, organic reactions, and polymers
  • JEE / EAMCET foundation tests, formula revision, and grand test series

BiPC - Biology, Physics, Chemistry

Best for medicine, pharmacy, nursing, agriculture, biotechnology, B.Sc life sciences, nutrition, and allied health careers.

First Year Subjects
  • English and second language
  • Botany: plant kingdom, morphology, anatomy, cell biology, and plant physiology basics
  • Zoology: animal diversity, structural organization, biology in human welfare, and ecology basics
  • Physics: units, motion, laws of motion, heat, waves, and optics basics
  • Chemistry: atomic structure, bonding, states of matter, solutions, and organic basics
  • Botany, Zoology, Physics, and Chemistry practical records with diagram practice
Second Year Subjects
  • Botany: genetics, molecular biology, plant physiology, biotechnology, and ecology
  • Zoology: human anatomy, physiology, reproduction, genetics, evolution, and applied biology
  • Physics: electricity, magnetism, modern physics, electronics, and communication systems
  • Chemistry: thermodynamics, electrochemistry, coordination compounds, biomolecules, and organic reactions
  • NEET / EAMCET practice tests, diagrams, short notes, and chapter-wise revision
Commerce Curriculum

Commerce stream groups

CEC - Commerce, Economics, Civics

Best for B.Com, BBA, law, public administration, civil services foundation, banking, and management careers.

First Year Subjects
  • English and second language
  • Commerce: trade, business organization, entrepreneurship, and business services
  • Economics: demand, supply, production, cost, markets, and national income basics
  • Civics: constitution basics, rights, duties, citizenship, and government structure
  • Accountancy foundation: journal, ledger, trial balance, and final accounts practice
  • Business examples, chart work, and current affairs discussion
Second Year Subjects
  • Commerce: banking, insurance, marketing, consumer protection, and business finance
  • Economics: Indian economy, public finance, money, banking, planning, and reforms
  • Civics: political institutions, local governance, public policy, and administration
  • Accountancy: partnership accounts, company accounts, depreciation, and financial statements
  • CA Foundation basics, B.Com / BBA guidance, and commerce aptitude practice

MEC - Mathematics, Economics, Commerce

Best for finance, analytics, BBA, B.Com computers, CA/CMA foundation, banking, statistics, and business data careers.

First Year Subjects
  • English and second language
  • Mathematics: algebra, functions, trigonometry, matrices, statistics basics, and probability
  • Economics: microeconomics, demand, supply, production, markets, and national income
  • Commerce: business organization, trade, entrepreneurship, and business services
  • Accountancy foundation: journal, ledger, trial balance, and final accounts practice
  • Numerical practice, spreadsheet basics, and business problem solving
Second Year Subjects
  • Mathematics: calculus, probability, statistics, coordinate geometry, and applied business math
  • Economics: Indian economy, public finance, banking, planning, and economic development
  • Commerce: finance, marketing, business law basics, company forms, and consumer protection
  • Accountancy: partnership, company accounts, depreciation, and financial statements
  • CA/CMA foundation support, finance aptitude, and business analytics orientation
Arts Curriculum

Arts stream groups

HEC - History, Economics, Civics

Best for BA, law, civil services foundation, public administration, social work, teaching, and competitive exam preparation.

First Year Subjects
  • English and second language
  • History: ancient India, medieval India, modern India foundations, and cultural movements
  • Economics: basic concepts, demand, supply, production, markets, and national income
  • Civics: constitution, rights, duties, citizenship, and organs of government
  • Current affairs, map work, timeline preparation, and short-answer writing
Second Year Subjects
  • History: modern India, world history overview, freedom movement, and social reforms
  • Economics: Indian economy, planning, poverty, unemployment, public finance, and reforms
  • Civics: political institutions, local governance, public policy, and international relations basics
  • Civil services foundation, law entrance awareness, essay writing, and interview confidence

Humanities - History, Sociology, Public Administration

Best for journalism, psychology/social science degrees, teaching, media, public service, NGOs, and humanities research pathways.

First Year Subjects
  • English and second language
  • History: society, culture, reform movements, and historical interpretation basics
  • Sociology: society, family, community, social institutions, and culture
  • Public Administration: meaning, scope, organization, leadership, and public systems
  • Communication skills, note-making, debate, and presentation practice
Second Year Subjects
  • History: modern India, world events, social change, and national movement
  • Sociology: social change, social problems, research basics, and Indian society
  • Public Administration: bureaucracy, local administration, public policy, and welfare schemes
  • Journalism, law, civil services, teaching, and social-career mentoring
