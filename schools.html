Class 7 Curriculum

Subjects and development areas

  • English: grammar, reading comprehension, writing skills, poetry, and prose
  • Second language: reading, grammar, vocabulary, and written expression
  • Mathematics: integers, fractions, decimals, algebra basics, geometry, mensuration, and data handling
  • Science: nutrition, heat, acids and bases, motion, light, water, soil, and environmental awareness
  • Social Studies: history, geography, civics, maps, local government, and culture
  • Computer basics: typing, file management, internet safety, word processing, and presentations
Class 8 Curriculum

Subjects and development areas

  • English: formal writing, comprehension, vocabulary, grammar accuracy, and literature appreciation
  • Second language: grammar, essays, summaries, speaking practice, and cultural texts
  • Mathematics: rational numbers, linear equations, quadrilaterals, exponents, factorisation, graphs, and mensuration
  • Science: crop production, microorganisms, metals and non-metals, force, friction, sound, light, and conservation
  • Social Studies: modern history, resources, agriculture, industry, constitution, judiciary, and map skills
  • Computer applications: spreadsheets, presentations, coding basics, cyber safety, and simple projects
Class 9 Curriculum

Subjects and development areas

  • English: literature, grammar, analytical reading, notice writing, letters, and descriptive writing
  • Mathematics: number systems, polynomials, coordinate geometry, linear equations, triangles, statistics, and probability
  • Science: matter, atoms, tissues, motion, force, gravitation, work-energy, sound, and natural resources
  • Social Science: India and the contemporary world, democratic politics, economics, and physical geography
  • Information Technology: digital documentation, spreadsheets, communication skills, and employability basics
  • Assessment support: chapter tests, lab records, revision worksheets, and concept-remedial classes
Class 10 Curriculum

Subjects and development areas

  • English: board writing formats, literature, grammar, comprehension, and sample paper practice
  • Mathematics: real numbers, polynomials, quadratic equations, trigonometry, circles, statistics, and probability
  • Science: chemical reactions, acids-bases, metals, life processes, heredity, light, electricity, magnetism, and environment
  • Social Science: nationalism, resources, agriculture, political science, economics, maps, and case studies
  • Computer / IT: digital skills, documentation, data handling, online safety, and project submission
  • Board preparation: model papers, pre-final exams, revision timetable, practicals, and stream selection counseling
